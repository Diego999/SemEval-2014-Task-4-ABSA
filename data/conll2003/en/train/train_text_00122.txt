Ka Wah Bank sets HK$ 43 mln FRCD . 
HONG KONG 1996-08-22 
Ka Wah Bank 's HK$ 43 million floating rate certificate of deposit issue has been privately placed , sole arranger HSBC Markets said . 
The facility has a tenor of six months . 
It pays a coupon of 15 basis points over the six-month Hong Kong Interbank Offered Rate . 
Other details are not available . 
The deposit date is September 5 , 1996 . 
Clearing is through the Hong Kong Central Moneymarkets Unit .
