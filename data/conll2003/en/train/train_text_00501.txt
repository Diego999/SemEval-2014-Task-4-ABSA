ATRIA SEES H2 RESULT UP ON H1 . 
HELSINKI 1996-08-26 
Finnish foodstuffs group Atria Oy said in a statement on Monday it expects its result to improve in the second half of 1996 compared to the first half . 
" The result of the second year-half is expected to improve on the early part of the year , " Atria said . 
Atria said earlier its January-June profit before extraordinary items , appropriations and taxes fell to 15 million markka from 39 in the first-half of 1995 .
