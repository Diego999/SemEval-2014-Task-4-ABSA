SOCCER - FIORENTINA BEAT MILAN IN ITALIAN SUPERCUP . 
MILAN 1996-08-25 
Italian Cup winners Fiorentina beat 
league champions Milan 2-1 ( halftime 1-1 ) in the pre-season 
Supercoppa ( SuperCup ) in Milan on Sunday : 
Scorers : 
Fiorentina - Gabriel Batistuta ( 11th , 83rd ) 
Milan - Dejan Savicevic ( 21st ) 
Attendance : 29,582
