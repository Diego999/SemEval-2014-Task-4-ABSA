MOTOR RACING - SCHUMACHER WINS BELGIAN GRAND PRIX . 
SPA-FRANCOCHAMPS 1996-08-25 
Michael Schumacher of 
Germany , driving a Ferrari , won the Belgian Grand Prix motor 
race on Sunday . 
Canada 's Jacques Villeneuve finished second in his Williams 
and Mika Hakkinen of Finland was third in a McLaren . 
Frenchman Jean Alesi came fourth in his Benetton with 
Britain 's Damon Hill fifth in a Williams and Gerhard Berger of 
Austria sixth in the other Benetton .
