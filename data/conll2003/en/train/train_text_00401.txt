Dow rises on Philip Morris , other stocks lower . 
NEW YORK 1996-08-26 
The Dow Jones industrial average opened slightly higher on Monday , boosted by Philip Morris , which gained four to 92 . 
Other shares were slightly lower , mirroring bonds . 
The Dow was up eight to 5,731 , while the NASDAQ Index was off fractionally to 1,143 and the S&P Index down one to 666 . 
New York Stock Exchange advances lagged declines by 476/698 while NASDAQ advances led declines 837/763 . 
The 30-year U.S. Treasury bond was off 2/32 to yield 6.96 percent .
