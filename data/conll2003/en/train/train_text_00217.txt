SunGard to buy CheckFree unit . 
SAN MATEO , Calif. 1996-08-23 
SunGard Shareholder Systems Inc , a subsidiary of SunGard Data Systems Inc , said it had entered into a definitive agreement to buy the Securities Products Business unit of CheckFree Corp . 
The company said in a statement on Friday the deal was expected to be finalized in September . 
Terms were not disclosed . 
The purchase is not expected to have a material effect o SunGard 's financial condition or results of operations .
