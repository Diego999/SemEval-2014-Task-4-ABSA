Chad parliamentary election set for November 24 . 
N'DJAMENA 1996-08-29 
Chad 's President Idriss Deby has signed a decree fixing November 24 as the date for parliamentary elections , state radio said on Thursday . 
Nomads will vote at mobile polling stations around the vast Central African country between November 20 and 24 . 
The electoral commission said the new 125-member national assembly would be installed on February 10 . 
Deby took power in an armed uprising in 1990 . 
He won 69 percent of votes in the second round of presidential elections on July 3 , 1996 .. 
His supporters will contest the parliamentary election in a coalition of 30 mainly small parties , the Republican Front , led by Deby 's Patriotic Salvation Movement .
