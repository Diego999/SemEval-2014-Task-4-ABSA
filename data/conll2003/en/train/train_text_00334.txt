CRICKET - ENGLAND NAME SQUAD FOR ONE-DAY INTERNATIONALS . 
LONDON 1996-08-25 
The England cricket squad was announced on Sunday for the one-day international series against Pakistan starting on Thursday . 
Squad : Michael Atherton ( captain ) , Alec Stewart , Graham Thorpe , Nick Knight , Graham Lloyd , Matthew Maynard , Ronnie Irani , Adam Hollioake , Robert Croft , Darren Gough , Peter Martin , Dean Headley , Alan Mullally .
