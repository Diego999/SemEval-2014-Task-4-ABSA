Hungary 's gross foreign debt rises in June . 
BUDAPEST 1996-08-23 
Hungary 's gross foreign debt rose to $ 27.53 billion in June from $ 27.25 billion in May , the 
National Bank of Hungary ( NBH ) said on Friday .
