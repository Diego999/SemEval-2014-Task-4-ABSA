Japan 's Hashimoto leaves Brazil for Peru . 
BRASILIA 1996-08-26 
Japanese Prime Minister Ryutaro Hashimoto left Brasilia on Monday for Lima , the penultimate stop on a 10-day Latin American tour , a Brazilian Foreign Ministry spokeswoman said . 
Hashimoto , who has already visited Mexico and Chile , spent three days in Brazil . 
After Peru , he was due to go to Costa Rica .
