SOCCER - TOGO BEAT CONGO 1-0 IN AFRICA NATIONS CUP QUALIFIER . 
LOME 1996-08-25 
Togo beat Congo 1-0 ( halftime 0-0 ) 
in their African Nations Cup preliminary round , second leg 
qualifying match on Sunday . 
Scorer : Salou Bachirou ( 53rd minute ) 
Attendance : 18,000 
Togo win 1-0 on aggregate .
