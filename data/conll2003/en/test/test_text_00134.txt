LUXEMBOURG CHRISTMAS MARKET GOES ON WORLD WIDE WEB . 
BRUSSELS 1996-12-06 
Luxembourg 's traditional Christmas market , which starts on Saturday and runs to December 24 , has taken to the world wide web as a way of publicising its activities . 
The web site ( http://www.pt.lu/infoweb/kreschtmaart ) gives details of the market 's concert programme as well as its various retailers .
