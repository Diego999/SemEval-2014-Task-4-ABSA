SOCCER - WEAH HEAD-BUTT DEPRIVES PORTUGAL OF COSTA . 
LISBON 1996-12-06 
Portugal called up Porto central defender Joao Manuel Pinto on Friday to face Germany in a World Cup qualifier in place of injured club colleague Jorge Costa , who is still nursing a broken nose after being head-butted by Liberian striker Georg Weah . 
Costa has not played since being struck by the AC Milan forward after a bad-tempered European Champions ' League game on November 27 . 
Portugal lead European qualifying group nine with seven points from four games , one more than Ukraine and three more than Germany , who have only played twice . 
The Portuguese host Germany on December 14 .
