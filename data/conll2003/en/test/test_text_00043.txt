SOCCER - SPAIN PICK UNCAPPED ARMANDO FOR WORLD CUP CLASH . 
MADRID 1996-12-06 
Spain coach Javier Clemente has added uncapped Deportivo Coruna midfielder Armando Alvarez to his squad for the World Cup qualifier against Yugoslavia on December 14 . 
" I do n't believe it ... 
I thought it was a joke , " said Armando who replaces injured Atletico Madrid playmaker Jose Luis Caminero .
