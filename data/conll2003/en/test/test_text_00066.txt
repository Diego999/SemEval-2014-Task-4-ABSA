RTRS - Cricket - Play restarts in Australia-West Indies match . 
MELBOURNE 1996-12-06 
Play restarted in the first World Series limited overs match between West Indies and Australia after a rain delay of 50 minutes on Friday . 
West Indies resumed their innings on 53 for two with opener Sherwin Campbell on 25 and Shivnarine Chanderpaul 10 . 
Rain earlier delayed the start of play by 30 minutes .
