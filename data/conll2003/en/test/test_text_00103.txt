China to open port in Hainan to foreign ships . 
BEIJING 1996-12-06 
China 's State Council , or cabinet , has given a port in the southern province of Hainan permission to open to foreign vessels , the Xinhua news agency said on Friday . 
Xinhua did not say when Qinglan port in Wenchang city would be opened to foreign vessels . 
Wenchang has built a berth for 5,000 deadweight-tonne container ships at the port and invested 34 million yuan ( $ 4.1 million ) to dredge the harbour , Xinhua said . 
It gave no further details . 
( $ 1 = 8.3 yuan )
