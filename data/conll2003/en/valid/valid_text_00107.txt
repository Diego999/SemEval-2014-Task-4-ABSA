SOCCER - SLOVAKIA BEAT FAROES IN WORLD CUP QUALIFIER . 
TOFTIR , Faroe Islands 1996-08-31 
Slovakia beat the Faroe Islands 2-1 ( halftime 1-0 ) in their World Cup soccer European group six qualifying match on Saturday . 
Scorers : 
Faroe Islands - Jan Allan Mueller ( 60th minute ) 
Slovakia - Lubomir Moravcik ( 13th ) , Peter Dubovsky ( 88th ) 
Attendance : 1,445 .
