SOCCER - ROMANIA BEAT LITHUANIA IN UNDER-21 MATCH . 
BUCHAREST 1996-08-30 
Romania beat Lithuania 2-1 ( halftime 1-1 ) in their European under-21 soccer match on Friday . 
Scorers : 
Romania - Cosmin Contra ( 31st ) , Mihai Tararache ( 75th ) 
Lithuania - Danius Gleveckas ( 13rd ) 
Attendance : 200
