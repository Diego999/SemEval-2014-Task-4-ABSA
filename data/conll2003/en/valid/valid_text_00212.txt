Nato declines comment on fighting in Iraq . 
BRUSSELS 1996-08-31 
The North Atlantic Treaty Organisation 's spokesman on Saturday declined all comment on reports of armed conflict in northern Iraq . 
But a NATO official told Reuters : " We are watching the situation closely . " 
Earlier on Saturday , an Iraqi Kurd leader said both Iraqi troops and Kurdistan Democratic Party ( KDP ) forces were attacking the city of Arbil in northern Iraq .
