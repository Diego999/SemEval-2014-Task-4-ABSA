SOCCER - U.S. BEAT EL SALVADOR 3-1 . 
LOS ANGELES 1996-08-30 
The United States beat El Salvador 3-1 ( halftime 1-0 ) in an international soccer friendly on Friday . 
Scorers : 
U.S. - Joe-Max Moore ( 3rd minute , 88th on penalty kick ) , Eric Wynalda ( 61st ) 
El Salvador - Luis Lazo ( 61st )
