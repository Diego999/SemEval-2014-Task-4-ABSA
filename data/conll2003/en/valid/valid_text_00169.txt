SOCCER - ROMANIA BEAT LITHUANIA IN U-21 QUALIFIER . 
BUCHAREST 1996-08-30 
Romania beat 2-1 ( halftime 1-1 ) Lithuania in their European Under-21 soccer match on Friday .

Romania - Cosmin Contra ( 31st ) , Mihai Tararache ( 75th ) 
Lithuania - Danius Gleveckas ( 13rd )
