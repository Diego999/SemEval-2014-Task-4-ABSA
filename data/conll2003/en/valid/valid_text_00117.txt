SOCCER - WALES BEAT SAN MARINO IN WORLD CUP QUALIFIER . 
CARDIFF 1996-08-31 
Wales beat San Marino 6-0 ( halftime 4-0 ) in a World Cup soccer European group 7 qualifier on Saturday . 
Scorers : Dean Saunders ( 2nd minute , 75th ) , Mark Hughes ( 25th , 54th ) , Andy Melville ( 33rd ) , John Robinson ( 45th ) . 
Attendance : 15,150
